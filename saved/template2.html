TWERKSHOP * Friday August 22nd

Flight Risk Aerials is hosting a TWERKING WORKSHOP, instructed by Jasmine Beg, on August 22nd from 7:00-8:00pm!

Come with an open mind and ready to burn calories and have a lot of fun! Proper attire is shorts or pants that are non-constricting and comfortable, sneakers, leggings and/or knee pads.
