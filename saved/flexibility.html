Flight Risk Aerials Flexibility Classes

Flexibility Classes in Morristown NJ

Do your muscles feel tight or sore? Do you want to improve your flexibility for different poses? Whether you are a dancer, or an individual, who just desires a relaxing session, this class will help you achieve results! You will be surprised at what your body is capable of achieving through consistent practice. This fun class will take you through a series of dynamic and static stretches, which will help you feel relaxed, rejuvenated, and burn calories. It is always enjoyable and more motivational to stretch with others. We welcome all ages, so get ready to meet others with the same passion!

Drop-ins welcome for all our Morristown NJ Aerial Silks Classes - there's no need to sign up or register, try any class anytime! All of our classes are $20 for a drop in, $75 for a package of 4 classes or $130 for a package of 8 classes. Pay the instructor when you get to class with cash or a check, or click here to register online or pay the drop-in fee with a credit card. We also offer an unlimited monthly classes membership for $150.

What To Wear To Flexibility Class

Dress in comfortable, stretchy and breathable clothes, with bare feet.
