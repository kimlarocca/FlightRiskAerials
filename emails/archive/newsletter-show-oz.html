Don't forget - our awesome $10 STUDIO SAMPLER class will be at 7pm on December 5th (the 1st Friday of every month)! Our Studio Sampler class includes instruction on Pole Dancing and Aerial Silks. Students will be able try out different types of pole and silks moves, practice, or ask questions on how to learn a specific move. This class is not as formally taught as our other classes, but will include a group warmup and instruction on all the different types of equipment. No prior pole or aerial experience is necessary to join this class!
