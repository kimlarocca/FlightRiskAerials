Twerkshop Next Friday!

Flight Risk Aerials is hosting a TWERKING WORKSHOP, instructed by Jasmine Beg, on Friday November 21st from 7:30-9:00pm!

The one hour workshop will consist of stretching, drills and choreography. Twerking is a high intensity workout that will get your heart pumping. Come with an open mind and ready to burn calories and have a lot of fun! Proper attire is shorts or pants that are non-constricting and comfortable, sneakers, leggings and/or knee pads. Water and a towel are recommended.
 

Pre-registration is required to reserve your space. Spaces are limited. If you are under 18 years of age, you will need a legal guardian to sign a consent form.


Cost: $25
When: Friday November 21st at 7:30pm
Where: Flight Risk Aerials in Morristown NJ
