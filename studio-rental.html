Rent Our Dance & Fitness Studio!

Our dance studio is available for rent on an hourly basis at a rate of $25/hr ($50/hr for Friday & Saturday evenings only). Our studio is fully equipped with mirrors and a sound system that can play music from Pandora Radio, YouTube & other internet websites, CDs, iPods/iPhones & other MP3 players. Please text us at 862-823-2778 to book the studio! We are generally available for rentals during weekdays, on Fridays and on Sundays. Other times may also be available, just ask!
