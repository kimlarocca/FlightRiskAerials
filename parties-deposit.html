Private Parties Deposit & Terms

Please pay for your private party deposit ($100) after you have read the following terms & conditions carefully:

 

1. The balance must be paid upon arrival with cash, credit card or a check made out to Flight Risk Aerials. Our private parties are $200/hr for groups of 6 or less, or $30/person/hour for groups of 7 or more. Non-instructional studio rentals are $50/hr.

 

2. Please arrive 10 to 15 minutes early to ensure that your party begins on time. We often have multiple parties on any given night with 15 minutes of buffer time in between, so we won't be able to run late. Late parties will still be responsible for the full payment even if the full hour (or more) of reserved time is not used.

 

3. If your party is more than 15 minutes late, the instructor has the right to refuse your party or leave the premises. In this event, your deposit will be forfeited in order to cover the studio rental as well as the instructor's time.

 

4. If it is your first time coming to our studio, you will need to sign our waiver prior to arriving. Download it at http://www.flightriskaerials.com/waiver.pdf, print it out and bring signed copies with you. If participants are under 18 then you'll need a parent/guardian to sign their waiver. If you fail to bring the signed waiver, then you can sign them when you arrive. That entails registering in our online system and signing a digital waiver, which can be time consuming when there are a lot of people. Please be advised that signing waivers will take up your allotted lesson time.

 

5. Due to insurance reasons, we don't allow alcoholic beverages during the lesson, but if you can rent the studio after your lesson if you'd like extra time to party! (Rent the studio for non-instructional time at a rate of $50/hour). Please be advised: Due to safety concerns, if you arrive under the influence the instructor has the right to refuse your party. In this event, your deposit will be forfeited in order to cover the studio rental as well as the instructor's time.

 

6. If you have any special requests or instructions, please let us know before your arrival (e.g., if you want us to be discreet regarding payment in front of the bachelorette / birthday girl / guest of honor).

 

7. All lotion must be removed from all body parts before class as it ruins the poles & silks for other students and instructors. All jewelry including wedding and engagement rings must be removed before class as it will damage our equipment and your jewelery will also get damaged. If someone in your party refuses to remove jewelery, the instructor has the right to bar their participation in the lesson.

 

8. Please note that we have limited equipment available. For example, we normally have 6 poles and 8 silks. However, we do not guarantee equipment being available at any given time because occasionally the equipment needs to be replaced for safety reasons.

 

9. This deposit must be paid in full in order to ensure your party's date & time is reserved. This deposit is fully refundable if you cancel at least 48 hours in advance. If you cancel less than 48 hours in advance, your deposit will be forfeit in order to cover the cost of the studio rental as well as the instructor's time.

 

10. Please text us at images the day of your party if you have NOT heard from us with a confirmation.
