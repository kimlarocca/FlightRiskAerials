Bollywood Dance Classes

* we are currently not offering this class - sorry!

This Bollywood dance class will include a range of Indian dance styles such as Indian classical, bhangra, folk dancing combined with contemporary movements like hip hop, jazz, latin and belly dancing depending on the music.

class="margin20"The class will begin with warm up, followed by choreography. During the warm ups we will focus on developing techniques, facial expressions and grace of Indian classical dance (Bharatnatyam and Odissi). We will be working on eye and head movements, complex footwork and mudras (hand gestures). During the choreographed lessons we will be exploring and incorporating the elements learned during the warm ups into semi-classical Bollywood choreography while some classes will focus on high-energy music from Bollywood movies that lean more towards Bhangra and folk dances instead of classical.
