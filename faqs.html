Registering / Signing Up

Do I need to sign up or register? Equipment is limited for Aerial Silks, Lyra and Pole Dance, so drop ins for these classes are on a first-come first-serve basis! Please reserve your spot by visiting our register online page. There is no need to register, sign up or notify us in any way if you plan to attend bellydance class.

Is there space available for me to attend class? Yes, there are usually still spots available the day before class and often the day of class. Please reserve your spot by visiting our register online page. There is no need to register, sign up or notify us in any way if you plan to attend bellydance class.

When does the next session start? We do not have "sessions" for our classes. You can join any of our classes at anytime!

I would like to attend one of your regularly scheduled classes with a group of friends. Is that ok? Equipment is limited for Aerial Silks, Lyra and Pole Dance, so we don't recommend that groups of 2 or more just show up. If you want to guarantee your spots, please reserve them by visiting our register online page. There is no need to register, sign up or notify us in any way if you plan to attend bellydance class.

 

I reserved my spot but can't make it. What do I do? You can visit our register online page to cancel upcoming registrations. Please text Amanda at 862-823-2778 if you need to cancel the day of - if the class is sold out we may not be able to refund your class fee.

 

I reserved my spot but am running late. What do I do? Please text Amanda at 862-823-2778 to let us know that you are on your way. If you reserve a spot and are more than 5 minutes late, we will give your spot away if we haven't heard from you and there is someone waiting.

First Time Students

I am a first time student. What do I need to know? If it's your first time coming to our studio, please arrive 10 minutes early! We don't want you to have to join the class late and we will need time to add you to our system and have you sign our waiver. Our classes often sell out, so you should reserve your spot by visiting our register online page. Please also plan for extra time needed to locate the studio. Click here for directions and parking information.

 

I am a first time or beginner pole/silks student. Which classes are appropriate? For your first time we recommend that you take our intro to pole dance, intro to aerial silks class, intro to lyra class or beginner bellydance classes - first timers are always welcome to join these classes!

Do I have to go to 4 or 8 consecutive weeks if I purchase a class package? No, you don't have to do 4 or 8 classes in a row. You can go to any class at anytime using the 4 or 8 class package, and you can use it for any class we offer.

Paying For Classes

How do I pay? You can pay for and reserve your spot by visiting our register online page. You can also pay the instructor with cash, credit or a check (made out to Flight Risk Aerials) when you arrive. Please note that if you do not register in advance, there may not be room in the class for you to attend.

If I pay for and reserve a spot online, what do I need to do? Once you reserve your spot by visiting our register online page, your name and info is automatically added to the attendance sheet for the class. All you need to do is show up!

Unlimited Monthly Memberships

What classes are included? Every class we offer is included in our unlimited membership program. You can go to as many classes as you want at anytime, just visit our register online page to reserve spots using your membership.

For example, if you purchase the membership on June 5th, your membership would be valid from June 5th through July 4th. What To Wear

Bellydance: Wear yoga or stretchy pants that allow free movement - no jeans! - and a shirt or tank top that is not too loose (we want to be able to see your movements). You can wear a cropped top and bare your stomach if you choose, but most students do not. You can dance barefoot or with socks. Most students also wear coin belts or hip scarves, which are available to buy or borrow at class.

Pole Dance: Comfortable workout clothing is advisable. For more intermediate moves, please wear shorts. Heels or sneakers are not allowed - most students pole dance barefoot, with socks or soft soled shoes. All lotion must be removed from all body parts before class as it ruins the poles for other students and instructors. All jewelry must be removed before class as it will damage our equipment and your jewelery will also get damaged.

Aerial Silks & Aerial Hoop (Lyra): Leggings, tights or stretchy pants that fit closely around the ankles are best along with a tight fitted T shirt, tank top or leotard. Bare feet. All jewelry must be removed before class as it will damage our equipment and your jewelery will also get damaged.

What To Bring

There's no need to bring anything! Some classes include a yoga-like warmup. We have yoga mats available to borrow, but feel free to bring your own if you prefer. For bellydance, bring a hip scarf of you have one! If not, we have hip scarves available to purchase or to borrow.

Health & Safety

Do I need to disclose any medical conditions? Yes. You will sign a wavier that requires you to disclose medical conditions that could affect your safety in class. For example: epilepsy, injuries (current or prior), etc.

 

Do you have safety mats? Yes we do! We encourage all students to use a safety mat. Safety mats are available at all times, just ask your instructor if you'd like to use one!

 

Is there a weight limit? Yes. The silks can hold about 1000lbs. The poles are rated for up to 250lbs.

Students Under 18

I'm between 15 and 18 years old - can I attend your classes without a parent? Yes - but if you're under 18 then you'll need a parent/guardian to sign a waiver the first time if you wish to attend aerial silks, lyra or pole dance classes. Your parent/guardian can sign it when you get there, or click here to download our waiver and bring it with you!

I'm under 15 years old - can I still take your classes? There's no age minimum, but for students under 13 we require a parent to take the class along with them to be sure they are ok and help if needed. If that's cool then feel free to reserve 2 spots in the class you'd like to take! Kids can take subsequent classes by themselves if the instructor approves.

My kids want to try one of your classes. Is that ok? There's no age minimum, but for students under 13 we require a parent to take the class along with them to be sure they are ok and help if needed. If that's cool then feel free to reserve 2 spots in the class you'd like to take! Kids can take subsequent classes by themselves if the instructor approves.

Do you do aerial silks parties for kids? Yes we do! Please contact us to book your party or visit our private parties page for more information.
