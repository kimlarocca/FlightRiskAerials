Bellydance Class Descriptions

Beginner Bellydance

Calling all bellydance beginners!!! Join this class anytime if you want to learn the ancient art of bellydancing. This class is ideal for first timers and beginners, and even more advanced students who want to brush up on basics. Our normal warm up consists of muscle toning exercises and yoga based stretching. After the warm up we move onto drills, which include arms, shoulders, chest, stomach, and hips. We do the same warm up and drills each week. After our warm up and drills we work on a combination or choreography. You can join this class at anytime - beginners are welcome! More advanced students will also benefit from this class, and it's a great complement to any of our other classes.

Advanced Beginner Bellydance

Open to anyone with about 6 months of belly dance experience.

Cabaret Style Bellydance

Learn cabaret style belly dance through technique drills and fun choreography!

Take your technique to the next level with this mixed level raqs sharqi class. Cabaret style bellydance, or "raqs sharqi", uses traditional Turkish and Egyptian movements. This class is designed to improve basic technique as well as enhance your movement vocabulary through more complex drills and combinations. A perfect class for any beginner or intermediate level dancer.

Tribal Fusion Bellydance

The style of bellydance taught in this class is Tribal Fusion Bellydance - choreographed American Tribal Style Bellydance fused with other related dance styles including American Nightclub/Cabaret Bellydance, Indian Classical Dance, Bollywood, Flamenco and Hip Hop. Learn (and perform, if you choose) complete tribal fusion bellydance choreographies, some of which include veils and other props. Open to beginners and more advanced students. Our mixed level classes can be fast paced at times. Basic bellydance knowledge is helpful but not required. Beginners are welcome as long as you are comfortable and feel you can keep up!

Bellydance Revolution Dance Crew Rehearsal

This class is by invite only and is open to those students in our bellydance classes. Please talk to Kim if you are interested in joining! We do Tribal Fusion Bellydance (see above) and Hip Hop Bellydance: blending hip hop moves such as waving, liquid, popping, strobing, tutting and gliding/floating with bellydance creates a powerful and fun style of dancing. Learn and perform complete tribal fusion and hip hop bellydance choreographies similar to those seen on America's Best Dance Crew. Basic hip hop dance knowledge is helpful but not required. Basic bellydance skills are required.

View more bellydance videos on the Bellydance Revolution YouTube Channel!

What To Wear To Bellydance Class

Wear yoga or stretchy pants that allow free movement - no jeans! - and a shirt or tank top that is not too loose (we want to be able to see your movements). You can wear a cropped top and bare your stomach if you choose, but most students do not. You can dance barefoot or with socks. Most students also wear coin belts or hip scarves, which are available to buy or borrow at class.
